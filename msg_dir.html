Message from the director


The RESEARCH & MANAGEMENT CENTRE (RMC) of Al Madinah
International University was established by the University
Senate in 2017. The main objective of its establishment is to
promote, coordinate and enhance research, publication, training
and consultancy activities of the University in line with the
vision of MEDIU in promoting Islamic-based knowledge globally.
It aims to become a leading international reference centre to
promote applied research which seeks to restore the
dynamics and progressive role of the Muslim Ummah in all
branches of knowledge. The Research Management Centre is
located in the main campus at Taman DesaPetaling in Kuala Lumpur.
